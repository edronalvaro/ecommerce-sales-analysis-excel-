# ecommerce-sales-analysis-excel-

## Overview
This project analyzes U.S. e-commerce sales data to uncover insights on product performance, revenue trends, and customer behavior. The analysis was performed entirely in Microsoft Excel using pivot tables, formulas, and dashboards.

The goal is to simulate real-world business analysis by transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights for decision-making.

---

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle – United States E-Goods Sales Dataset
- Type: Synthetic e-commerce transactional data
- Contains information on:
  - Orders and dates
  - Product
  - Customer details (city, state, etc.)
  - Quantity and price

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Data Cleaning Functions
- Formulas (SUMIFS, IF, XLOOKUP)
- Dashboard Design

---

## Data Preparation
Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned and structured:
- Removed or handled missing values
- Standardized categorical fields (regions, categories)
- Checked for duplicate records
- Ensured correct numeric formatting for revenue and profit fields
- Created calculated fields for deeper analysis

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### Revenue Performance
- Total revenue by product, state, region, month, and hour
- Identification of top-performing product and regional segments

### Customer and Regional Insights
- Sales distribution across states and regions
- Identification of high-performing regions

### Product Analysis
- Best-selling products by quantity and revenue
- Low-performing products with weak sales contribution

### Time-Based Trends
- Monthly revenue trends
- Seasonal spikes in sales activity
