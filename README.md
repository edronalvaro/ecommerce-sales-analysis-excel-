# ecommerce-sales-analysis-excel-

## Overview
This project analyzes U.S. e-commerce sales data to uncover insights on product performance, revenue trends, and customer behavior. The analysis was performed entirely in Microsoft Excel using pivot tables, formulas, and dashboards.

The goal is to simulate real-world business analysis by transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights for decision-making.

---

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle – United States E-Goods Sales Dataset
- Type: Synthetic e-commerce transactional data
- Contains information on:
  - Orders and dates
  - Product
  - Customer details (city, state, etc.)
  - Quantity and price

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel

## Key Insights

### Premium Electronics Drive Revenue
MacBook Pro Laptops, iPhones, and ThinkPad Laptops generated the largest share of total revenue, indicating that business performance is highly dependent on high-ticket technology products. Maintaining inventory availability for these products is critical to revenue growth.

### California Is the Dominant Market
California generated approximately $13.7M in revenue, significantly outperforming every other state. This suggests that customer demand is heavily concentrated in this market and may warrant additional investment in targeted promotions and customer acquisition strategies.

### Strong Seasonal Demand During Q4
Revenue and order volume peaked in Q4, with December producing the highest monthly sales. This highlights the importance of seasonal demand planning, inventory management, and promotional campaigns during the holiday period.

### Customer Purchasing Activity Peaks During Midday and Evening Hours
Sales activity was strongest between 12 PM and 9 PM, indicating that customers are most engaged later in the day. Marketing campaigns and promotional offers may be more effective when scheduled during these peak shopping hours.

### The West Region Leads Overall Performance
The West region generated the highest total revenue, driven largely by California's performance. Understanding the factors behind this regional success could help inform expansion and marketing strategies in other regions.

### Accessory Products Present Cross-Selling Opportunities
Although accessories generated lower direct revenue than premium electronics, they are complementary purchases that can be bundled with high-value products to increase average order value.

## Business Recommendations

### Prioritize High-Revenue Products
Ensure sufficient inventory levels for top-performing products such as MacBook Pro Laptops, iPhones, and ThinkPad Laptops, as these products generate a significant portion of total revenue.

### Increase Marketing During Peak Sales Periods
Allocate additional marketing and promotional resources during Q4, particularly October through December, when customer demand and order volume are highest.

### Focus Growth Efforts in High-Performing Markets
Leverage California's strong sales performance by testing new marketing campaigns, promotions, and customer retention strategies before expanding them to other markets.

### Optimize Promotion Timing
Schedule email campaigns, digital advertisements, and promotional offers during midday and evening hours when customer purchasing activity is highest.

### Implement Product Bundling Strategies
Bundle accessories such as headphones, charging cables, and batteries with premium electronic products to increase average order value and improve overall revenue per transaction.
