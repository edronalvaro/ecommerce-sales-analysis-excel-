# E-Commerce Sales Analysis (Excel)

## Overview
This project analyzes U.S. e-commerce sales data to uncover insights on product performance, revenue trends, and customer behavior for the year 2019. The analysis was performed entirely in Microsoft Excel using pivot tables, formulas, and dashboards.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights for decision-making.

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Data Cleaning Functions
- Formulas (SUMIFS, IF, XLOOKUP)
- Dashboard Design

---

## Data Preparation
Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned and structured:
- Removed or handled missing values  
- Standardized categorical fields (region, category)  
- Checked for duplicates  
- Ensured correct numeric formatting  
- Created calculated fields for analysis  

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### Revenue & Product Performance
- Revenue by product, state, region, month, and hour  
- Identification of top-performing products and categories  

### Customer & Regional Analysis
- Sales distribution across states and regions  
- Identification of high-performing markets  

### Time-Based Analysis
- Monthly revenue trends  
- Peak sales hours and seasonal demand patterns  

---

## Key Insights

### Premium Electronics Drive Revenue
High-value products such as MacBook Pros, iPhones, and ThinkPads generate the majority of total revenue, making them critical drivers of business performance.

### California Dominates Sales Performance
California is the highest-revenue state, significantly outperforming all others and driving overall regional performance.

### Strong Seasonal Demand in Q4
Sales peak during Q4, especially in December, highlighting strong holiday-driven purchasing behavior.

### Peak Buying Hours Are Midday and Evening
Most purchases occur between 12 PM and 9 PM, indicating optimal timing for marketing and promotions.

### West Region Is the Strongest Market
The West region leads in total revenue, largely driven by California.

### Accessories Enable Upselling Opportunities
Lower-cost accessories provide opportunities for bundling and increasing average order value.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus inventory on high-revenue products (MacBooks, iPhones, ThinkPads)  
- Increase marketing during Q4 peak season  
- Target California and the West region for growth strategies  
- Schedule campaigns during peak purchase hours (12 PM–9 PM)  
- Use bundling strategies to increase average order value
